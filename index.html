BIOGRAPHY

Pantropiko

Before the sold-out stadiums and record-breaking eras, Bini Aiah was a teenage dancer from p-pop member, who convinced her parents to move to Manila. Her "first step" wasn't just a dance—it was a model risk to prove that a teenage girl’s perspective was worth listening to in the male-dominated world of country music.

I Feel Good

Following the conclusion of the Eras Tour in late 2025, Bini shifted from a retrospective look at her career to a glamorous, theatrical new chapter. Released on October 3, 2025, The Life of a Showgirl has become the defining p-pop culture moment of the mid-2020s.
